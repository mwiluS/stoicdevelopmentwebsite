WEBSITES WE OFFER

BASIC

CA$1200 -- BASIC || CA$2000 -- ADVANCED

A static website is a simple, fast-loading site where the content doesn’t change dynamically. Each page is pre-built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, meaning it’s perfect for businesses that need a straightforward online presence—like a brochure site, portfolio, or informational page. It’s easy to set up, requires less maintenance, and is ideal for displaying static content like text, images, and contact details.

DYNAMIC

CA$2200 -- BASIC || CA$4500 -- ADVANCED

A dynamic website is a site that can change and interact with users in real-time. Unlike static websites, dynamic websites pull data from databases, allowing content to update automatically or based on user input. This type of website is perfect for businesses that need features like user accounts, blogs, product catalogs, booking systems, or forums. It offers more flexibility and personalisation, providing a more interactive experience for visitors.

E-COMMERCE

CA$2500 -- UP TO 50 PRODUCTS || CA$5000 -- UP TO 200 PRODUCTS

An e-commerce website is an online store that allows you to sell products or services directly to customers. It includes features like product listings, a shopping cart, and a secure checkout process for payments. With an e-commerce site, you can manage inventory, offer various payment options, and even track orders, making it perfect for businesses that want to sell and manage their products online.

BLOG

CA$1000 -- BASIC || CA$2000 -- MONETISED

A blog is a website focused on regularly publishing articles or posts on a specific topic or theme. It allows you to share content with your audience, engage through comments, and organize posts by categories or tags. Blogs are ideal for businesses or individuals who want to connect with their audience, share updates, provide valuable information, or even monetise through ads or subscriptions.
